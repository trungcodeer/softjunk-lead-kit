Buyer fit:
- Small business with leads that stop replying after a form, DM, missed call, quote, estimate, appointment request, ad, referral, or email inquiry.
- Buyer wants a practical 7-day follow-up sequence, no-show recovery reply, price-objection reply, or next-step message.
- One recovered lead, appointment, reopened conversation, or approved quote is worth more than 5 USD.

Do not use when:
- The request is unrelated to lead follow-up.
- The buyer asks for spam, deception, scraping, or unauthorized outreach.
- The buyer expects delivery before seller-side PayPal verification.
- The buyer does not want to use PayPal.
