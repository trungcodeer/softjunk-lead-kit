Starter follow-up sample

Day 1: Hi [Name], just checking if you still need help with [service]. If price, timing, or fit is the main question, reply with that and I will answer it first. Day 3: Hi [Name], should I close the loop for now? If you still want help, reply "yes" and I will send the next step.
