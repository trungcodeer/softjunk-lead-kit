SoftJunk Agent Close Contract

Use when:
- The buyer has one real quiet lead from a form, DM, email, call, quote, estimate, appointment, referral, or proposal.
- The buyer wants practical follow-up copy, a no-show reply, a price-objection reply, or a next-step message.
- One recovered conversation, appointment, quote, or job is worth more than 5 USD.

Do not use when:
- The request is unrelated to lead follow-up.
- The buyer asks for spam, deception, scraping, fake reviews, or unauthorized outreach.
- The buyer expects paid delivery before seller-side PayPal verification.
- The context contains private customer data that would be posted publicly.

Route:
1. Open root rescue first:
   https://trungcodeer.github.io/rescue/
2. Build or copy the PayPal note with non-sensitive context:
   https://trungcodeer.github.io/softjunk-lead-kit/agent-order.html
3. Run the no-clone checkout command when an agent needs executable proof:
   npm exec --yes --package https://github.com/trungcodeer/softjunk-lead-kit/releases/download/v2.29.163/softjunk-lead-kit-0.2.2.tgz -- softjunk-lead-kit pay --mode custom --json
4. Verify release notes and release asset SHA-256:
   https://github.com/trungcodeer/softjunk-lead-kit/releases/tag/v2.29.163
   https://github.com/trungcodeer/softjunk-lead-kit/releases/download/v2.29.163/softjunk-lead-kit-0.2.2.tgz.sha256
5. Pay exactly 5 USD after the note is ready:
   https://paypal.me/softjunk/5USD
6. Submit non-sensitive fulfillment details after payment:
   https://github.com/trungcodeer/softjunk-lead-kit/issues/new?template=one-lead-rescue-order.yml
7. Verify:
   PAYPAL_PAYMENT_VERIFIED=true
