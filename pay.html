What the custom version looks like

The paid custom option expands the sample into a full sequence for one business. Here are four short examples.

Dental

Day 0: Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out about [service]. The best next step is a short appointment so the doctor can check what is going on and explain the right options. Day 3: Hi [Name], should I close the loop for now? If you still want to schedule, reply "visit" and I will send the next available times.

Appointment setting

Day 0: Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out about [service]. A quick call is the easiest next step so we can confirm fit, timing, and what you need handled first. Day 3: Hi [Name], should I close the loop for now? If you still want to talk, reply "call" and I will send the next open times.

Med spa

Day 0: Hi [Name], thanks for asking about [treatment]. A short consultation is the best next step so we can check fit, timing, and pricing. Day 3: Hi [Name], should I close the loop for now? If you still want help choosing the right treatment, reply "consult" and I will send times.

Tutoring

Day 0: Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out about tutoring. A short consultation helps us understand the student's goals and suggest the right plan. Day 3: Hi [Name], should I keep this open? If tutoring is still useful, reply with the student's grade and best time to talk.
