Examples for appointment businesses

The custom option is strongest when the next step is clear: book, reply, confirm, or ask for pricing. Here are two common cases.

Med spa booking inquiry

Lead source: Instagram DM. Next step: book a consultation.

Day 0: Hi [Name], thanks for asking about [treatment]. The best next step is a quick consultation so we can check fit, timing, and pricing for your goals. Day 1: Hi [Name], just checking if you still wanted help choosing the right [treatment]. I can send booking times or answer one question first. Day 3: Hi [Name], should I close the loop on this for now? If you want to come back later, reply "consult" and I will send the booking link again.
