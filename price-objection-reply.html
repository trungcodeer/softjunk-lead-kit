Price reply sample

Hi [Name], totally fair to compare options. If price is the main issue, I can show the smallest useful version first or explain what is included so you can decide. Would it help if I sent the lower-scope option?

Why price objections need a careful reply

Arguing loses the lead

A strong reply acknowledges the concern, keeps trust intact, and offers a smaller decision instead of defending the whole price.

The buyer may still be qualified

Price hesitation can mean the buyer needs scope, timing, or value explained more clearly. The custom version writes that path for one real service.

The next step should be easy

The goal is to get one useful reply: budget, timing, scope, or approval. The $5 sequence gives Day 0, 1, 3, 5, and 7 follow-ups.
